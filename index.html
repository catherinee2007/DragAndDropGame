Remarks:

Totalcore:

Objective: Drag each animal image to the correct name to score points.

How to Play: Click and hold the animal image you wish to match. Drag the image over to the corresponding name. Release the mouse button to drop the image onto the name.

Scoring: You will earn points for each correct match. Incorrect matches will not receive points, but you can try again.

Tips: Take your time to think about each animal and its name. Use hints if available to help you with difficult matches.
